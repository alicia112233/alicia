About HOTBAKE studio

  • At HOTBAKE studio, we provide many engaging and interactive hands-on baking classes during the holidays which allow participants (individuals or as a family) to gain experience of the entire baking journey.
  • Additionally, we take part in yearly charity events, whereby we sell our foodies to raise funds for organizations and beneficiaries.
  • Our classes range from beginner level (baking enthusiasts with little/no baking experience), to advanced level classes (baking kings to enhance their techniques).
  • Every lesson is organised to impart baking skills, advice and useful tips!
  • Furthermore, after spending an enjoyable and memorable day baking at our new studio, all participants get to bring home their own tasty bakes!
  • To view on the classes we offer, scroll down for the table of classes!
